## üé® Customizing Your Blog

### Change Your Site Title & Info

1. Go to **"Site Settings"** (left sidebar)
2. Click **"Configuration"**
3. Update:
   - **Site Title:** Your blog's name
   - **Description:** What your blog is about
   - **Your Name & Email**
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Change Colors (Make it Yours!)

In the same "Configuration" page:

1. Scroll down to **"Theme Colors"**
2. Enter new color codes (these are called "hex codes")
3. Click **"Publish"**

**Popular Color Schemes:**

| Theme | Primary Color Code | What it looks like |

Just copy-paste the code into "Primary Color"!
